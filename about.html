Welcome

Welcome to Build Fast Deliveries (Pty) Ltd!

Who we are

Build Fast Deliveries (Pty) Ltd is a Durban-based business that provides quick and dependable delivery of building supplies to contractors, homeowners, and construction sites.

We are committed to providing excellent service and ensuring that our customers receive their building materials in a timely and efficient manner.

Why we were founded

Build Fast Deliveries was established to solve transportation issues in the building industry. To satisfy the increasing demand in cities like Durban, the company concentrates on offering quick, secure, and reasonably priced delivery services.

What we deliver

We specialize in delivering a wide range of building materials, including cement, bricks, sand, and lumber. Our mission is to ensure that your construction projects run smoothly by providing timely and reliable delivery services.

Our commitment

We take pride in our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction.

How we support you

Our team is dedicated to ensuring that your building materials are delivered on time and in perfect condition, allowing you to focus on your construction projects without worrying about logistics.
