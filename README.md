1.1 Brief History 

Build Fast Deliveries (Pty) Ltd was established in 2022 in Durban, KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa. The company was created to provide reliable and efficient transportation services for building materials to contractors, construction companies, hardware stores, and individual homeowners. 

As the construction industry continues to grow, the demand for fast and dependable delivery services has increased. Build Fast Deliveries specializes in transporting materials such as cement, bricks, sand, stone, roofing materials, timber, and other construction supplies directly to project sites. 

The company's focus is on timely deliveries, customer satisfaction, and dependable logistics solutions that help construction projects stay on schedule.
